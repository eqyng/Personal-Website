How do we shape technology,
and how does technology shape us?

This is a question I've been asking myself for a while now.

My work explores how we design and relate to digital systems through the lenses of psychology, systems thinking, and long-term ethics. To me it seems like the more we delve into machines, the more we reveal about the human condition. And about how little we actually know ourselves.

Right now, I design conversations at Versa.ai.

Career-wise, my path has moved across disciplines: from studying psychology to research, from copywriting to digital marketing, from change management to community design. While roles have varied, a few things remain consistent so far:

- I enjoy wrestling with systems and structures

- I try to optimise language into concise forms

- Emerging tech startup have been my playgrounds
