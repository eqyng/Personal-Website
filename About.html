How do we shape technology,
How does technology shape us?

I've shaped my career around this question, exploring the intersection of human behaviour and digital systems in some form - psychology, marketing, community design, conversational AI.

The roles change but the through-line is the same: Helping organisations build technology that honors human dignity.

I currently work as an AI Conversation Design Consultant, focusing on ethical implementation balanced with business realities. My work involves ensuring that flows are safe by design, and not as an afterthought. I ensure organisations navigate that complexity well.<>

The AI ethics field needs practitioners able to bridge technical implementation and humanistic wisdom. The deeper we move into building intelligent machines, the more we confront how little we understand ourselves. This tension is where I live.


My approach combines:

- Systems thinking and structural analysis - locating what's broken, designing better

- Linguistic precision that underpins the complexity of culture

- Long-term considerations that account for tomorrow


I work with:

- Organisations building responsible conversational AI

- Teams navigating ethical AI implementation

- Creative collaborators exploring technology and humanity

My practice spans speaking engagements (speaking on red-teaming conversational AI agents), published writing (including a poetry collection on childhood) and direct consulting work.

Work with me on AI Ethics or follow my writing on technology and humanity
